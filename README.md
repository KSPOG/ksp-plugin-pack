# ksp-plugin-pack
Here i'l be adding all the plugins i made & will make.




# Plugin Info

- KSPAutoMiner: Does what its named after trains mining also has a progressive option.
- ksplamps or Lampent: The moment a Exp Lamp appears in your inventory it'll use the lamp on the skill of your choice.
- BalancedAttackSyle: Switches combat style to keep your melee stats balanced (usefull when using plugins that don't switch atk styles).
